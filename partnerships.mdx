Partnerships are for people and teams who can help Bulls Eye move.

We are open to smart launch support, not random logo swaps.

## Partnership lanes

- Community partnerships
- KOL and media support
- Builder and tooling integrations
- Launch distribution support
- Liquidity and market support

## Good fit

- You have real reach
- You have real distribution
- You can help launch clean
- You understand the Robinhood Chain crowd

## Not a fit

- Fake engagement
- Empty promo promises
- Lazy paid shills with no actual reach
- Generic “partnership” messages with no plan

## What to send

- Who you are
- What audience you reach
- What support you can bring
- What you want in return
- Timeline and contact details

## Reach out

If you want to work with Bulls Eye, contact the team through the official channels with a real proposal.
