---

Launch Access is the early round before public trading opens.

It is there to help seed LP, support rollout, and bring in early aligned size before the full public launch.

## How it works

1. The round opens.
2. Wallets send native ETH to the early access contract.
3. Contributions are tracked on-chain.
4. The round closes and the final `BEYE` claim pool is locked in.
5. Contributors claim their pro-rata `BEYE` from that pool.

## What matters

- The contribution contract is public
- The raised ETH helps seed LP and rollout
- The claim pool is fixed after the round closes
- Bigger contribution share means bigger claim share
