## Chainlink VRF

Every eligible buy is run through Chainlink VRF.

The draw result is determined through the contract flow and can be verified on-chain.

## On-chain receipts

The important path is public:

- an eligible buy comes in
- Chainlink VRF resolves the draw
- the result can be checked on-chain
- if it wins, the payout goes out in native ETH

If the chain says it happened, it happened.

## Liquidity plan

LP will be locked for `3 months` at launch.

After a successful launch, the LP will be burned forever.

## Why this matters

The whole point is to make Bulls Eye feel simple:

- buy
- qualify
- draw
- verify

That is the trust model.
