Launch Access is for approved contributors who want early on-chain access before public trading opens.

This is not a public sale page. It is a private access lane for wallets helping support launch liquidity and early momentum.

## What it is

- Limited whitelist access
- Approved wallets only
- On-chain purchase access before the public open
- Designed for contributors helping seed launch strength

## What it is not

- Not an open public presale page
- Not a promise of profit
- Not a free-for-all round with unlimited allocation

## Who this is for

- Contributors helping seed LP
- Serious early supporters
- Buyers who want approved launch access without chasing the public open

## How it works

1. Reach out for review.
2. We review fit, size, and wallet plan.
3. Approved wallets are added to the launch access list.
4. Those wallets get early on-chain buy access under the launch rules.

## What matters

- Access is limited
- Access is reviewed
- Access can be capped per wallet
- Final launch terms are set by the team

## Apply

If you want Launch Access, contact the Bulls Eye team directly through the official channels.
