Bulls Eye is simple on purpose.

Buy `BEYE`. Clear the minimum. Take your shot. If you hit, the contract pays native ETH straight to the wallet that bought.

## The pitch

- Every qualified buy is its own draw
- Bigger buys get bigger odds
- Winners get paid in native ETH
- Only half the vault is live as jackpot, so there is always ammo left
- The whole thing can be checked on-chain

## At a glance

- Name: `Bulls Eye`
- Ticker: `BEYE`
- Total supply: `1,000,000,000`
- Buy tax: `5%`
- Sell tax: `5%`
- Qualifying buy floor: starts at `0.01 ETH`

## Why people will care

Most tax tokens feel dead the second you buy them.

Bulls Eye keeps the action in front of everyone. The jackpot is visible. The result is visible. The payout is visible. You do not have to guess where the tax is going.
