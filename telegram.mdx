# Telegram

Bulls Eye uses Telegram to surface jackpot activity in a way the community can follow easily.

## What the bot does

- Posts qualifying buy results
- Highlights winners
- Posts jackpot reminder messages
- Links users to on-chain verification

## Why it matters

The Telegram feed is not the source of truth.

The blockchain is the source of truth.

The bot exists to make the result flow easier to follow in real time, especially during active trading periods.

## Reminder messages

Jackpot reminder posts can be used to:

- keep attention on the live jackpot
- point users to buy and chart links
- reinforce the public game loop around the vault

## Media

The bot can run text-only or use attached media later for stronger winner and non-winner messages.
