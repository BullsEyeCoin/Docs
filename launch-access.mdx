Launch Access is the early round before public trading opens.

It is built to help seed LP, support rollout, and bring in early aligned size before the full public launch.

## What it is

- Open contribution round
- Native ETH goes on-chain into the early access contract
- Final `BEYE` claim pool is set after the round closes
- Claims are split pro-rata based on contribution size

## What it is not

- Not a promise of profit
- Not a fixed-rate sale where the final token amount is known up front

## Who this is for

- Contributors helping seed LP
- Serious early supporters
- Buyers who want to get positioned before public trading opens

## How it works

1. The early access round opens.
2. Wallets send native ETH to the early access contract.
3. Contributions are tracked on-chain.
4. The round closes and the final `BEYE` claim pool is locked in.
5. Contributors claim their pro-rata `BEYE` from that pool.

## What matters

- The contribution contract is public
- The raised ETH helps seed LP and rollout
- The claim pool is fixed after the round closes
- Bigger contribution share means bigger claim share

## Apply

If you want in, use the official Early Access page and only send from the wallet you want credited on-chain.
