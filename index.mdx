# Bulls Eye

Bulls Eye is a Robinhood Chain token built around a simple idea: every qualified buy gets a chance to hit a live jackpot funded in native ETH.

The project is designed to keep the game visible on-chain:

- Qualified buys take a shot at the jackpot
- Larger buys receive larger weighted odds
- Winners are paid in native ETH
- The jackpot is always half of the available vault
- Results can be verified on-chain

## At a glance

- Name: `Bulls Eye`
- Ticker: `BEYE`
- Total supply: `1,000,000,000`
- Buy tax: `5%`
- Sell tax: `5%`
- Minimum qualifying buy: starts at `0.01` native ETH equivalent

## Why Bulls Eye exists

Most tax tokens route value into wallets that traders never feel connected to.

Bulls Eye routes trading activity into a visible jackpot system. Instead of hiding the value flow, the project centers the vault, the jackpot, and the result path as part of the product itself.
