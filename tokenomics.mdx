Bulls Eye keeps the setup tight. No bloated split. No mystery buckets.

![Tokenomics chart](./images/tokenomics-breakdown.svg)

## Identity

- Name: `Bulls Eye`
- Ticker: `BEYE`
- Total supply: `1,000,000,000`

## Allocation

- `80%` liquidity allocation
- `10%` team allocation
- `5%` marketing
- `5%` treasury

The pie chart shows the full starting supply split at launch.

## Launch policy

- LP will be locked
- Team allocation will be locked

## Taxes

- Buy tax: `5%`
- Buy split: `4%` jackpot and `1%` vault
- Sell tax: `5%`

Taxes are owner-adjustable, but both buy tax and sell tax are hard-capped at `10%`.

## Buy odds

- Minimum qualifying buy starts at `0.01` native ETH equivalent
- Larger buys receive larger weighted odds
- Odds can be tuned by the owner if the game needs balancing

![Buy odds graph](./images/buy-odds-curve.svg)

## Jackpot policy

- Only qualifying buys trigger jackpot entries
- Winners are paid in native ETH
- The jackpot is always half of the available vault balance
