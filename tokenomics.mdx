- After a successful launch, the LP will be burned forever
- Team allocation will be locked

## Taxes

- Buy tax: `5%`
- Sell tax: `5%`

Both sides feed the vault. During bonding that grows the pot. After migration, only qualifying buys can roll for the jackpot.
