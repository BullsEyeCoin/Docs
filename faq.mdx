## What is the token name?

`Bulls Eye`

## What is the ticker?

`BEYE`

## What chain is Bulls Eye launching on?

Robinhood Chain.

## What is the total supply?

`1,000,000,000 BEYE`

## What are the taxes?

- Buy tax: `5%`
- Buy split: `4%` jackpot, `1%` vault
- Sell tax: `5%`

Both are owner-adjustable, but capped at `10%`.

## Do sells trigger jackpot entries?

No. Only buys can take a shot.

## What is the minimum qualifying buy?

The starting floor is `0.01 ETH`, and the owner can adjust it if needed.

## Can bigger buys get better odds?

Yes. Bigger buys get heavier odds.

## How are winners paid?

Winners are paid in native ETH from the jackpot vault.

## Will LP be locked?

Yes. Public launch messaging should state that LP will be locked.

## Will the team allocation be locked?

Yes. Public launch messaging should state that the team allocation will be locked.
