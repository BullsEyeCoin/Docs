## What is the token name?

`Bulls Eye`

## What is the ticker?

`BEYE`

## What chain is Bulls Eye launching on?

Robinhood Chain.

## What is the total supply?

`1,000,000,000 BEYE`

## What are the taxes?

- Buy tax: `5%`
- Sell tax: `5%`

Both buy tax and sell tax feed the jackpot side and the rewards vault side. Only qualifying buys roll for the jackpot.

## Is every jackpot draw verifiable?

Yes. Every eligible buy is run through Chainlink VRF, and the result path can be checked on-chain.

## What is the LP plan?

LP will be locked for `3 months` at launch. After a successful launch, the LP will be burned forever.

## Do sells trigger jackpot entries?

No. Only buys can take a shot.

## What is the minimum qualifying buy?

The starting floor is `0.01 ETH`.

## Can bigger buys get better odds?

Yes. Bigger buys get better odds, based on the native ETH value of the buy.

## What is the highest launch odds per buy?

At launch, the curve tops out at `10%` on a `1 ETH` buy.

## How are winners paid?

Winners are paid in native ETH from the jackpot vault.
